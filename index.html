Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does routine dry washing reduce a garment's lifespan?

    Routine cleaning increases the lifespan of a garment. Regular dry cleaning not only refreshes your clothes but also helps maintain their quality and longevity.

  • Can I dry clean clothes marked as washable?

    The term "washable" on a garment's care label does not guarantee safe dry cleaning. It's crucial to follow care instructions to ensure the best maintenance for your items.

  • Do MYVE Dry Cleaners in Ram Nagar, Coimbatore use bleach?

    Bleach is a last resort at MYVE Dry Cleaners. We only use it when absolutely necessary and when we're certain it won't harm the item. Your garments are treated with the utmost care and consideration.

  • Can all stains be removed by dry cleaning?

    While dry cleaning is more effective than washing machines in stain removal, not all stains can be eliminated. If you're dissatisfied with the results, communicate with MYVE Dry Cleaners, and we'll address your concerns.

  • How long does MYVE Dry Cleaners in Ram Nagar, Coimbatore take to process orders?

    MYVE Dry Cleaners strives to deliver within 48 hours of pick-up. Please note that unique orders may require additional processing time. We prioritize efficiency without compromising on the quality of our services.
